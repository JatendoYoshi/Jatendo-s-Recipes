Banana Cake

Ingredients

You will need these following ingredients:

  • 3 ripe Bananas

  • 1 1/2 cup Plain Flour/All Purpose Flour (180g)

  • 1 tsp Baking soda

  • 1/2 cup Butter (Soften according to your prefrence)(115g)

  • 1/2 cup Granulated Sugar (100g)

  • 1/4 cup Brown Sugar (45g)

  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract

  • 2 Eggs

  • 1/3 cup Milk (80mL)

  • 1/4 tsp Salt

Instructions

  1. Mash the bananas until it has form a liquid with lumps

  2. Then, in a big bowl, siv in the flour, baking soda and salt. Then leave aside for further use.
  3. Prepare another big bowl. Put in the butter, granulated sugar and brown sugar. Mix together until it forms a mashed potato texture.

  4. In your bowl of butter, add in the vanilla extract and eggs, then mix together.

  5. Then, mix in the milk into your bowl of wet mixture.

  6. Pour in the mashed bananas in your wet mixture, then mix it throughly.

  7. Finally, put in the dry ingredients.

  8. Put your final mixture into an oven-safe mold/container, then bake for 55-60 mins

  9. And there it is, a nice, tasty Banana Cake!
